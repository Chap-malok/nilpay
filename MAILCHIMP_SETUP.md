# Mailchimp Setup Instructions

Follow these steps to connect Mailchimp to your NilPay waitlist form.

## Step 1: Create Mailchimp Account

1. Go to https://mailchimp.com/
2. Click **Sign Up** (top right)
3. Enter your email and create password
4. Complete verification
5. Create a new account (select "I'm a new user")

## Step 2: Create Your First Audience (Mailing List)

1. In Mailchimp dashboard, click **Audience** (left sidebar)
2. Click **Create Audience**
3. Fill in details:
   - **Audience name:** NilPay Waitlist
   - **Company name:** NilPay
   - **Website:** https://chap-malok.github.io/nilpay
   - **Industry:** Technology
   - **From email:** noreply@nilpay.com (or your email)
4. Click **Create Audience**

## Step 3: Get Your Form Embed Code

1. In Mailchimp, go to **Audience** → **Manage Audience** → **Signup Forms** → **Signup Forms**
2. Click **Embedded Forms**
3. Click the form (should be one created by default)
4. Copy the embed code

## What Happens When Someone Joins:

1. User enters email → submits form
2. Mailchimp captures the email automatically
3. Email appears in your Mailchimp Audience
4. You can send campaigns and automations to them
5. They get a confirmation email (optional)
