4. **Click "Design"** to create the email content

Use Mailchimp's drag-and-drop builder:
- Add a **Header** with your NilPay logo
- Add text explaining your announcement
- Add a **Button** linking to your site or app

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Track Analytics

Monitor who's opening your emails and clicking your links.

### View Campaign Analytics:

1. Go to **Campaigns** (left menu)
2. Click on any campaign you've sent
3. Scroll down to see:
   - **Opens:** How many people opened your email
   - **Clicks:** How many clicked your links
   - **Bounces:** Emails that failed to deliver
   - **Unsubscribes:** People who unsubscribed

### Key Metrics to Watch:

| Metric | What It Means | Good Range |
|--------|---------------|-----------|
| **Open Rate** | % who opened your email | 15-25% |
| **Click Rate** | % who clicked your link | 2-5% |
| **Bounce Rate** | % of invalid emails | <3% |
| **Unsubscribe Rate** | % who left your list | <0.5% |

### View Audience Analytics:

1. Go to **Audience** (left menu)
2. Click on **NilPay Waitlist**
3. Click **Dashboard**
4. See:
   - Total subscribers (growing)
   - Chart of subscriber growth
   - Recent activity
   - Top countries/locations

### How to Improve:

1. **Low Open Rate?**
   - Test different subject lines
   - Send at different times
   - Keep subject under 50 characters

2. **Low Click Rate?**
   - Make button text more compelling
   - Use clear call-to-action
   - Test different button colors

3. **High Unsubscribe Rate?**
   - Don't email too frequently
   - Provide valuable content
   - Respect subscriber preferences
